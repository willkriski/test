When project updates live only in commit messages, most of the context disappears. A news-style site gives each release, experiment, and milestone a place to breathe.

This template uses Jekyll posts so every new article is just a Markdown file. That keeps the workflow simple while giving the homepage enough structure to feel editorial.

## Why this format works

- Featured stories help your most important update lead the page.
- Article cards make smaller notes feel intentional instead of buried.
- A dedicated post layout gives each piece room for richer writing.

You can use this structure for build logs, AI notes, changelogs, tutorials, or short essays tied to your GitHub work.
