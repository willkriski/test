Launch days create a lot of related content at once: announcements, screenshots, dev notes, follow-ups, and postmortems. A magazine-style homepage gives all of that content structure.

Instead of burying everything in one long stream, you can dedicate space to the main announcement and then support it with smaller pieces in the surrounding columns.

## What that structure does

- keeps the top story obvious
- gives supporting coverage room to breathe
- makes the site easier to scan on desktop and mobile
