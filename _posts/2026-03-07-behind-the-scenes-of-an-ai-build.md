Great project pages do more than list features. They explain the choices, rough edges, and moments where an idea changed direction.

That is where a post-driven layout becomes useful. Instead of forcing everything into a single landing page, you can publish smaller updates as the work evolves.

## A strong editorial rhythm

Readers usually want three things:

- What changed
- Why it matters
- What comes next

This template is designed to support exactly that rhythm without adding a CMS or a database.
