One of the nicest things about a magazine layout is that it supports different article sizes. Some stories deserve a full feature, while others work better as short notes.

That mix makes the homepage feel active. It also makes publishing easier because you can post quick updates without redesigning the page every time.

## A useful rhythm

Try mixing:

- one lead feature
- two or three medium stories
- several short links or notes
